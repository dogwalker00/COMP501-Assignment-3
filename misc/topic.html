The page or area describing your chosen technology/topic and why it is significant and/or has important implications for society.

When deciding on your chosen technology/topic, it is best not to choose something too broad, but limit the focus e.g. consider the level of social analysis from the figure below.

Our topic is Robotics in Medicine and Healthcare, the reason why we have chosen this field is because of the
technological advancements in recent years of robots in many different industries, and we have interest in
healthcare as well, to improve patient outcome in the health sector. The combination of these two fields into
one topic allows us to explore both of these interesting topics for our project.

Medicine and healthcare are very important for our society, it handles treatment, diagnosis, prevention
of ailments, and the promotion of wellbeing. Overall Healthcare is very important for our society, it improves
quality of life of many individuals with chronic health conditions (e.g. diabetes), and it help supports people
who have physical or mental health needs. It allows people to be producive despite of underlaying aliments,
allowing them to contribute to society and it's wellbeing. With the usage of Robotics within this field it
allows for far better accurate diagnosis of people with hidden health conditions, with Ai-assisted imaging.
This will reduce the amount of people who need to deal with and be held back by their health condition, Thus
reducing the productivity/contribution within their own society.

Healthcare could also increase equity between individuals within the society, some people have health defect
from when they were born. With Healthcare it allows individual such like these a chance to live fulfilling
fulfillinf lives, and address health disparities in society.
Lastly healthcare system reflects on the cultural values of the society, values of care, compassion and
support of one another. The further easer access of healthcare services shows us as a society prioritize
values of taking care of each other and well-being.
